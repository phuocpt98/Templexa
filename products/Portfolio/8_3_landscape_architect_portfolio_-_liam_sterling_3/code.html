About Julian

The Photographer

Photography is more than capturing light; it's about preserving the raw, unspoken emotion of a moment. With over a decade of experience in editorial and portrait photography, I strive to create images that feel both timeless and urgently present.

My methodology is grounded in observation. I prefer to step back, allowing the scene to unfold naturally before stepping in to frame it. Whether in the controlled environment of my studio or the unpredictable chaos of an outdoor shoot, my goal remains the same: to find the quiet truth in every subject.

I work exclusively with medium format film and high-resolution digital back systems to ensure that every texture, shadow, and highlight is rendered with absolute fidelity.
