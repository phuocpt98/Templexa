Girl Meets Boy

It began with a chance encounter at a local coffee shop in the Marina District. Marcus accidentally picked up Elena's almond milk latte, and she wasn't shy about correcting him. What started as a mix-up turned into a conversation that lasted for hours, spanning topics from architecture to obscure 80s music. Five years, three apartments, and one adopted golden retriever later, we are ready to embark on our greatest adventure yet. We invite you to join us as we celebrate our love and commitment to one another.
