Biography

Visual storytelling is about finding the truth in the shadows. With over a decade of experience behind the lens, I specialize in crafting atmospheres that speak louder than dialogue.

Based in London and working globally, my approach blends technical precision with an intuitive grasp of natural light. Whether for commercial narratives, music videos, or feature films, my goal remains the same: to evoke emotion through imagery.

When I'm not on set, I'm experimenting with analog film formats and studying the master painters of the Baroque period for their use of chiaroscuro.
