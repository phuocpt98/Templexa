# Vietnamese Wedding Invitation Market Research Report

**Date:** 2026-03-25
**Scope:** Wedding invitation customization requirements, customer data fields, features, and B2B partner processes

---

## Executive Summary

Vietnamese wedding invitation market (thiệp cưới) is dominated by digital solutions (thiệp cưới online, thiệp cưới điện tử). Key platforms include iWedding, Vesey, Chung Đôi, CineLove, Nắm Tay (Nắmtay), and TheSimple. Wedding invitations in Vietnam are highly customizable, with distinct cultural requirements (two-sided design for bride/groom families). Essential features include RSVP management, guestbook/congratulations collection, QR-based gift collection (tiền mừng), and guest analytics.

## 2. Wedding Invitation Sections/Components

### 2.1 Standard Vietnamese Wedding Invitation Layout

Vietnamese invitations typically follow a **2-part structure**:

#### Left Side (Bride's Family / Nhà Gái)
1. **Opening/Envelope** (Phần bao thiệp)
   - Decorative header/border
   - "Chân thành kính mời" (Cordial invitation) or opening greeting

2. **Bride's Family Announcement** (Báo tin nhà gái)
   - Bride's parents' names (as hosts)
   - Bride's name
   - Event type: "Lễ vu quy" (Bride receiving ceremony) or "Tiệc cưới" (Wedding reception)

3. **Left Side Event Details**
   - Date and time
   - Venue location

#### Right Side (Groom's Family / Nhà Trai)
1. **Groom's Family Announcement** (Báo tin nhà trai)
   - Groom's parents' names (as hosts)
   - Groom's name
   - Event type and timing

2. **Right Side Event Details**
   - Date and time
   - Venue location

#### Additional Standard Sections
- **Closing message/invitation line** (Kính mong bạn tham dự)
- **Special requests** (Yêu cầu đặc biệt)
  - Dress code (Mặc quần áo...)
  - "No gifts" policy (Chúng tôi không nhận quà)
  - Other protocol notes

### 2.2 Digital Wedding Invitation Sections (Online Features)

Beyond invitation text, digital platforms include interactive sections:

1. **Hero/Cover Section** (Trang bìa)
   - Couple photo or decorative background
   - Animated entrance screen
   - "Open" button/interaction

2. **Couple Information Section** (Thông tin cô dâu chú rể)
   - Bride and groom introduction
   - Couple photo/gallery

3. **Love Story Section** (Câu chuyện tình yêu)
   - Timeline of relationship
   - Meet story, proposal story
   - Photo moments

4. **Event Details Section** (Chi tiết sự kiện)
   - Ceremony details (date, time, venue, address)
   - Reception details (if separate)
   - Countdown timer to wedding

5. **Venue/Map Section** (Bản đồ địa chỉ)
   - Embedded Google Map
   - Directions
   - Parking information
   - Accommodation suggestions

6. **Gallery/Photos Section** (Thư viện ảnh)
   - Engagement photos
   - Couple memories
   - Pre-wedding shoots

7. **RSVP Section** (Xác nhận tham dự)
   - Guest name input
   - Attendance confirmation (Tham dự / Không tham dự)
   - Meal preferences
   - Number of guests
   - Special requirements/dietary restrictions

8. **Guestbook/Wishes Section** (Sổ lời chúc)
   - Guest congratulatory messages
   - Photo wishes (optional)
   - Public display of wishes

9. **Gift/Payment Section** (Phần tặng quà / Tiền mừng)
   - Gift registry link
   - QR code for bank transfer
   - Direct payment options
   - Gift wishlist

10. **Wedding Party/Entourage Section** (Thành phần đoàn)
    - Best man/maid of honor info
    - Wedding party members
    - Contact for coordination

11. **Schedule/Timeline Section** (Lịch trình ngày cưới)
    - Ceremony time and venue
    - Photo session time/location
    - Reception time and venue
    - Dance/cake cutting times

12. **Dress Code Section** (Yêu cầu trang phục)
    - Color theme guidance
    - Formal/smart casual specifications
    - Cultural dress options

13. **Additional Information Section** (Thông tin thêm)
    - Parking guidelines
    - Public transportation
    - Hotel recommendations
    - Contact numbers for day-of coordination

14. **Footer/Social Links** (Chân trang)
    - Social media links
    - Contact information
    - Website/hashtag for wedding updates

## 5. How Resellers/Partners Submit Orders

### 5.1 Partner/Reseller Engagement Models

Based on research, Vietnamese wedding platforms support **two engagement models**:

#### Model A: Direct Customer Self-Service
- Customer creates invitation directly on platform
- Customer manages and shares link
- Platform tracks all interactions and payments
- No intermediary involved

#### Model B: Reseller/Partner Ordering
- Reseller collects customer information via own form/process
- Reseller submits order to service provider
- Service provider creates custom invitation
- Service provider may provide reseller dashboard for tracking
- Reseller may white-label or brand as own service

### 5.2 Information Resellers Typically Collect & Submit

**Customer Information Packet Submitted:**
1. **Couple identification**
   - Bride name, groom name
   - Family names (both sides)
   - Parents' names (both sides)

2. **Event information**
   - Wedding date
   - Wedding time
   - Venue address (with detailed location)
   - City, district, ward
   - Special requests/dress code

3. **Visual assets**
   - Couple photo files (JPG/PNG)
   - Logo/watermark (if any)
   - Color theme preference code or hex values
   - Additional photo files for gallery

4. **Content**
   - Love story text (if custom)
   - Special invitation message (if custom)
   - Guest list (if pre-populated)
   - Social media handles
   - Wedding hashtag

5. **Financial/Gift details**
   - Bank account info
   - Gift registry URL
   - Payment method preferences

6. **Customization preferences**
   - Design template choice (by ID or name)
   - Color theme selection
   - Language preference
   - Music preference
   - Layout preferences (sections to include/exclude)
   - Number of revisions requested

7. **Reseller metadata**
   - Reseller/partner ID
   - Reseller order reference number
   - Order date
   - Delivery deadline
   - Contact person at reseller
   - Billing information

### 5.3 Common Reseller Order Submission Methods

**Research findings on reseller processes:**

1. **Google Sheet/CSV Templates**
   - TheSimple platform: Guest confirmation data saved directly to customer's Google Sheet
   - Data auto-exported for easy management

2. **Web Forms/Portals**
   - meWedding: Partners submit via web form with template design options
   - Customization details submitted in structured format

3. **Email + Attachment**
   - Provide design brief + customer info
   - Reseller sends photos, text, preferences via email
   - Service provider confirms via reply

4. **Reseller Dashboard**
   - Chung Đôi: Partner portal for bulk order management
   - Track order status, revisions, and delivery
   - Direct customer data entry or batch upload

5. **API Integration** (Advanced platforms)
   - For high-volume partners
   - Real-time order submission
   - Automated status tracking
   - Webhook notifications for updates

Reseller shares with customer →

## 6. Vietnamese Wedding Invitation Cultural Requirements

### 6.1 Structural Importance

**Two-Sided Design (Critical)**
- Vietnamese invitations MUST have distinct left/right or bride/groom sections
- Bride's family information on left or first
- Groom's family information on right or second
- Both families equally prominent/honored

**Parental Recognition**
- Parents' names placed FIRST and prominently
- Reflects cultural respect hierarchy
- Non-negotiable element in traditional Vietnamese weddings

### 6.2 Wording Conventions

**Standard Opening:**
- "Chân thành kính mời" (Cordial/Respectful invitation)
- "Kính thưa..." (Dear/Respected...)

**Closing/RSVP Line:**
- "Kính mong quý vị tham dự" (Respectfully hope for your attendance)
- "Vui lòng xác nhận tham dự" (Please confirm attendance)

**Family Names Format:**
- Families listed as hosts: "Ông X và Bà Y kính mời..." (Mr. X and Mrs. Y cordially invite)

## 8. Key Insights for Service Implementation

### 8.1 Market Expectations

1. **Ease of customization** - Non-technical users must be able to customize without coding
2. **Quick turnaround** - Customers expect invitations within 24-48 hours
3. **Unlimited revisions** - Competitive platforms include unlimited editing
4. **Mobile-optimized** - 80%+ of access is via mobile/QR scanning
5. **Multi-language** - At least Vietnamese + English expected
6. **Integration-ready** - Must connect with payment systems (bank transfer, digital wallets)

### 8.2 Revenue Opportunities (Research Context Only)

- Premium templates (higher margin)
- Add-on features: Music, custom video, advanced analytics
- Professional customization service (design team)
- Bulk orders from event organizers/resellers
- Extended hosting (beyond 1 year)
- Merchandise tie-ins (printed cards from digital design)

### 8.3 Competitive Advantages

- Pre-made templates with cultural authenticity
- Seamless RSVP + gift collection in one platform
- Automated guest analytics and reporting
- Multi-family support (coordinating groom and bride families)
- Professional design without designer hire
